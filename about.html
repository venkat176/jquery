ABOUT US

Xencia

In 2010, four of us left the comfort of our well-paid jobs in pursuit of fulfilling our dreams. “Xencia", meaning La excelencia in Spanish, was born to steer us in our goals. Our dreams eventually became the vision of Xencia. With multi-decades of technical experience in our kitty and a strong fervour to succeed, we embarked on a journey in to the world of technology. With experience and knowledge in delivery, we designed our Products and Services to leverage enterprises’ technology investments and multiply their benefits several folds.

Xencia’s headquarters is in Bangalore with offices in Chennai and Singapore. We work with multiple partners across the globe and has served over 100 customers across 12 different geographical locations. Xencia’s mission is to enable enterprises with smart and innovative solutions that are simple to deploy and augments business value in a short time capsule. Xencia focuses on addressing customer pain points rather than only selling and signing off with a goodbye. This helped building a lasting relationship with expanding clientele.

We are now one of the most preferred Microsoft Cloud Solution Provider for Azure services. We have attained Gold Competency for Cloud platform, Collaboration and Content, Small and Midmarket Cloud Solutions. Our engineers are Microsoft certified professionals in Azure, Office 365, SharePoint Development, SharePoint Server and Dot NET. We are one stop shop for all Azure services.
